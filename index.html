Create digital on-demand workforces that can do anything

OpenGuilds helps you build and manage online organizations that perform work for rewards you set.

Sign Up Sign In

Build a community

Guilds have different tasks and levels of responsibility. When you set up your guild, you control the flow of work.

Guilds can even use other platforms such as Amazon Mechanical Turk to provide an already existing community of workers you can have do the initial work that can be checked by more trusted members.

Send work in

Upload your initial work to be done and have it assigned to available members of your guild.

Guilds have an API developers can send data to. You can read more about our documentation here

Get finished work back

When the work is completed, we send it back to your service, with a complete history of who did what.

Workers are rewarded for completing the tasks correctly.
